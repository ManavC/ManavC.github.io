Project JoyBox

Project Info:

A student-led effort towards building a better tomorrow, project ‘Joybox’, is an initiative that involves making quality food available to vulnerable populations, while simultaneously improving the strength of local production systems. Through this project, we truly hope to earn the smiles of hundreds of hungry families and contribute towards the UN’s Sustainable development goal of ‘Zero Hunger by 2030’.

Project Details:

  • Reach:1850+ meals & 800 kgs of staples to communities in need; Installed community fridge; Raised 1000$; Learning Award Semi-Finalist at GSL.
