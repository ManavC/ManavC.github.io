WRO 2015 States

Challenge Info:

The name of this year's challenge is "Pearl Diving". This year's theme, "Robot Explorers", encourages students to build robots that can investigate and explore different environments, some of them hostile to humans. This game challenges you to build a robot that can dive and explore under the sea for pearls. For each dive you will only have 30 seconds before the robot needs to come back up for air

More Details:

  • Level:Elementary
  • Award Won:Participation
  • Technologies:EV3 Mindstorm - 4 Large motors, Block Programming, Line Following, Color sensing, Scissor and Dispensing Mechanism
  • Challenge Rules:2015 Elementary Rules
