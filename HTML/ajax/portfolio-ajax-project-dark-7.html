FTC Nationals

Challenge Info:

Two Alliances – one “Red” and one “Blue”, composed of two Teams each – compete in each Match. The object of the game is to attain a higher score than the opposing Alliance by locating and delivering Stones and Skystones from the Loading Zone to the Building Zone, building the highest Skyscraper, and placing the Capstone on the Skyscraper. The game is played in two distinct periods: Autonomous and Driver-Controlled

More Details:

  • Level:National
  • Award Won:Control Award, First Pick Alliance, Runner-Up Alliance
  • Technologies:Mecanum wheel,Tetrix & REV components, Compliant Wheel variable Intake, 5 skystone height extrusion, 8 motors, 4 Servos motors, Autonomous and Teleop code in Java, Block detection through camera
  • Challenge Rules:FTC Skystone Rules
