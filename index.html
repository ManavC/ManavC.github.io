HI! I'm Manav Chopda,

currently pursuing the IB curriculum at Aditya Birla World Academy. Creativity and Logic may not always go hand in hand but these two attributes are my greatest strength as they have seen me through extracurriculars like Chess and Robotics. I started playing Chess at the age of 4 years and participated in multiple State, National and International Tournaments and today I am a Rated Chess player.

Later, I found a strong passion for robotics. I have participated in various Robotic competitions like World Robot Olympiad(WRO), FIRST Lego League Challenge(FLL), FIRST Tech Challenge(FTC): Team Sigma #2119 & #20890 and most recently FIRST Robotics Competition(FRC): Team R-Factor #6024 where I have won numerous accolades. COMMUNITY SERVIVE LINE Imagining a robot in my mind then making it in CAD, constructing it and finally seeing it in action is a progress which I throughly enjoy. My hobbies apart from robotics are playing football and following F1 and the Premiere League.
